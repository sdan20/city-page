Visit Yuba City

Enjoy culture and sports in a little Nor*Cal city right by the famous Sutter Buttes

Top three activities to do in Yuba City

Experience local culture

The Sikh community hosts a delightful temple open house and parade every year, open to members of all faiths.

Head to the park

This local treasure is the best place to play a variety of sports, from pickleball to volleball to skateboarding.

Walk through city center

A safe place to spend time and get a bite to eat, now also home to a beautiful religious site--the Feather River Temple.
